My Recipie

FOOD PARADISE provides high quality, fast-served Asian-styled meals, based on a central theme of noodles. The meals cater for variety and are delivered fresh faster than other noodle-based products. The meals are provided to the consumer initially through mobile Noodle Vending Units (NVU’s), which provide a widespread point of sale coverage. The NVU’s are state-of-the-art, complying with health regulations. The NVU will become a product in itself, as FOOD PARADISE expands rapidly through franchising. FOOD PARADISE is a globally transportable business.

Balanced Meals

Balanced meals are made up of four key components: protein, carbohydrates, vegetables, and fat. To build a healthy meal, divide your dish into equal parts lean protein, complex carbohydrates, and fibrous veggies.

Prawn's Noodles

The prawn's noodles are wok-fried in a hot pan or wok to varying levels of crispness, and then tossed with a sweet and savory soy-based sauce that coats the noodles, transforming them from pale, lifeless yellow to a rich glowing amber. Crunchy bean sprouts, green onions, and carrot finish off the dish with added texture, flavor, and color.
